2012 Race Materials

The new branding and website I designed helped our team raise over $30,000 in profit for the 2012 race, which we donated to local charities. Our success led us to be rolled into a larger student planning organzation, which had specific requests for changes in the next year's design direction. The website also had to be rebuilt to be usable on phones - 40% of web traffic ended up coming from mobile devices on race day. Our collective efforts raised over $35,000 in charity profit in 2013.
